• Quarto is a two-player game with 16 unique pieces.
  • Each piece has four "attributes" that can be either "active" (one dot) or "passive" (two dots).
  • Player 1 starts by choosing a piece for Player 2 to place, and then vice-versa
  • The goal is to arrange four pieces in a line that share a common attribute.
  • The player that completes a line with a common attribute, wins.
  • If all pieces are placed with no winner, it's a draw.
